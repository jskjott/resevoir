At the depth of last winter A and I became aware of an option to gain a seasonal [[base]] in Denmark. On the 27th of June we arrived to a little coastal town some dozen kilometers away from my childhood home. As the sun went down with other paths once open to us, the gentle hands of a landscape well-known led us towards a space which would bring much embrace.
