## Last Harvest, for Ludum Dare 52

[Last Harvest](https://loooading.itch.io/last-harvest) is a tiny tower-defence inspired strategy game developed for the fantasy console Pico8 in 48 hours for Ludum Dare 52 in January 2023.

![screenshot from Pico8 game called Last Harvest, a green field is being taken over by a desert](images/ld52_out_of_memory.png)

## Scale Game

[[scale game]] is a puzzle platformer about a droplet which formed from the flow of water through the earths crust, dripping onto a coal spoil.
